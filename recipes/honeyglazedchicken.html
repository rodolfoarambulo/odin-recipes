Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bit-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook them up.
  3. Enjoy!
